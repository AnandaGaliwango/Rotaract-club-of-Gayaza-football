About Rotary Club of Gayaza

We are a diverse group of professionals and community leaders working together to create positive, lasting change in our local communities and around the world.

Our Mission

To provide service to others, promote integrity, and advance world understanding, goodwill, and peace through our fellowship of business, professional, and community leaders.

Our Vision

To create a world where people unite and take action to create lasting change â€” across the globe, in our communities, and in ourselves.

Our Values

Service, Fellowship, Diversity, Integrity, and Leadership guide our decisions and actions as we work to make a difference in our community.

Our Projects

We undertake various projects focused on improving lives in our community through sustainable initiatives.

Clean Water Initiative

Providing clean and safe drinking water to underserved communities in Gayaza through borehole installations and water purification systems.
